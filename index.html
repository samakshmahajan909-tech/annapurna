Our Story

About Annapurna Seva

Named after the goddess of nourishment, we are committed to alleviating hunger and providing support to vulnerable communities in Udhampur district.

Our Mission

Feeding Hope Daily

Every day, we serve nutritious meals at Government Medical College Udhampur, ensuring patients and their families never go hungry during difficult times.

Our Vision

A World Without Hunger

We envision a community where no one sleeps hungry, where compassion drives action, and where every meal served becomes a symbol of hope.
